# Pedal Lighting Remix
This work is based on the work done by [Pedal Lighting team](https://www.petallighting.com/)

# Spools
This is the part that helps you clean up the rig nicely, it holds all the wire for the lighting, and upon being deployed allows the base to be the stand for a tube.

## why the remix?
- This spool is designed to be be fully 3d printed - works on a prusa mk2.5s for size comparison but could likely fit on a slightly smaller printer too.
- The center of the spool is designed to be filled with cement to give the base more weight (my assembled spool is sitting at >700grams with the cement in place)
- Designed to hold my magnetic connector - but this could be remixed furthur to handle other sockets
- Opening in the base to allow the use of a speakon 4 pin connector

## Note
I haven't fully settled on the assembly for the magnetic connector in the sense that I might have to hollow out more space inside the connector as I'm likely going to add an RS485 module to convert the neopixel signal and allow longer cable length. no MCU needed just rs485 module and 5v power chip to convert from the 24v incoming power. becuase this change is likely happening the component `base connector bottom 000` is likely to get a revision
